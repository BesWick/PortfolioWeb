About me

Hello everyone! I’m a graduate from the University of California, Santa Cruz where I studied both Computer Science and Computer Networks. My background is primarily in backend web development and I have experience in app development, both in the Android and iOS space. I’ve managed and also built an iOS app for a client, and a digital closet app in Android Studio.

Along with my client projects, I’ve pursued my interest in backend technologies in my side projects, notably my compact Node.js based social web app and WeeChat, a ASP.NET MVC app written in C#.
